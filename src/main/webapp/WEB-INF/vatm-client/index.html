How does it work?

Need cash? Withdraw it from nearby shops!

Paymart introduces India’s first Virtual ATM (VATM) — no need to visit a physical ATM. Just head to a VATM-affiliated shop near you and complete your withdrawal securely and conveniently.

Steps to Withdraw Cash via VATM

  • 1. The customer initiates a transaction to withdraw cash using the bank's mobile app.
  • 2. The bank debits the customer's account and sends an OTP to their registered mobile number.
  • 3. The customer visits a nearby VATM-affiliated shop (merchant location) and provides the OTP along with KYC details.
  • 4. The merchant logs into VATM and validates the OTP.
  • 5. Upon successful verification, the merchant hands over the cash to the customer.
